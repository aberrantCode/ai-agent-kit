Architecture & Conventions

  • Verify dependencies in package.json before importing
  • RSC-safe: interactivity isolated in 'use client' leaves
  • Tailwind version lock · Grid over flex-math · min-h-[100dvh]
  • Emojis banned — Phosphor/Radix icons only

Bias Correction & Forbidden Patterns

  • The Lila Ban: no AI purple, no neon gradients, no Inter
  • No centered heroes at high variance · no 3-card rows
  • No generic names, fake numbers, or startup slop copy
  • Cards only when elevation communicates hierarchy

Creative Arsenal & Bento Engine

  • Liquid glass refraction · magnetic micro-physics
  • Staggered orchestration with spring physics
  • Bento 2.0: perpetual micro-interactions, 5 card archetypes
  • GSAP/ThreeJS isolated from Framer Motion trees

Performance Guardrails

  • Animate transform/opacity only — never top/left/size
  • Grain filters on fixed pointer-events-none layers only
  • Perpetual loops memoized in isolated client components
  • Z-index restraint: systemic layers only

Final Pre-Flight Check

Committed aesthetic direction · mobile collapse guaranteed · dvh-safe heights · effect cleanup · loading/empty/error states · cards omitted where spacing works · heavy animation isolated
